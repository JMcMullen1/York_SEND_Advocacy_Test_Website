Our Services

SEN Support & EHCP Guidance

We help parents and carers secure the right support at school. This includes guiding families through the Education, Health and Care Plan (EHCP) process – from initial requests to reviewing draft plans – ensuring the plan truly reflects the child's needs and legal rights.

Contact us to discuss SEN support

Mediation & Tribunal Support

Our advocates provide independent support during disputes with schools or the Local Authority. We assist with SEND mediation meetings and Special Educational Needs and Disability Tribunal appeals, helping you prepare evidence and understand your legal options throughout the process.

Contact us for tribunal assistance

Parental Coaching & Workshops

We offer one-to-one coaching and group workshops for parents and carers. These sessions empower families with practical strategies, peer support, and up-to-date knowledge of the SEND system, so you can confidently advocate for your child's needs.

Learn about upcoming workshops

Education Consultancy

Our experienced educators provide consultancy for complex situations – from choosing the right school placement to planning smooth transitions. We work alongside schools and professionals, advising on inclusive practices and helping develop effective support plans for students with SEND.
