Our Services

Comprehensive advocacy and support tailored to your family's needs.

How We Help

Expert SEND Support Services

We offer a range of services designed to help families navigate the SEND system with confidence.

📝

SEN Support & EHCP Guidance

We help parents and carers secure the right support at school. This includes guiding families through the Education, Health and Care Plan (EHCP) process, from initial requests to reviewing draft plans, ensuring the plan truly reflects the child's needs and legal rights.

Discuss your needs →
⚖

Mediation & Tribunal Support

Our advocates provide independent support during disputes with schools or the Local Authority. We assist with SEND mediation meetings and Special Educational Needs and Disability Tribunal appeals, helping you prepare evidence and understand your legal options.

Get tribunal help →
👥

Parental Coaching & Workshops

We offer one-to-one coaching and group workshops for parents and carers. These sessions empower families with practical strategies, peer support, and up-to-date knowledge of the SEND system, so you can confidently advocate for your child's needs.

View workshops →
🎓

Education Consultancy

Our experienced educators provide consultancy for complex situations, from choosing the right school placement to planning smooth transitions. We work alongside schools and professionals, advising on inclusive practices and effective support plans.

Get advice →
🏠

EOTAS & Alternative Provision

Bespoke Education Otherwise Than At School packages for young people who cannot attend mainstream settings. We coordinate multi-disciplinary teams and design personalised provision for learners with complex medical needs and neurodivergence.

Discuss EOTAS options →
💪

Bespoke 1:1 Interventions

Personalised therapeutic and educational interventions for EOTAS learners with complex needs, including trauma histories, school refusal and anxiety-based presentations.

Learn more →
✅

Safeguarding & Complex Casework

Specialist support for cases involving safeguarding concerns and their intersection with SEND. Our team has Designated Safeguarding Lead experience working with cases involving exploitation, domestic abuse and trauma, as well as CIN and CP processes.

Get support →
