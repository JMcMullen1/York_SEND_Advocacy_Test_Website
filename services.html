Our Services

Comprehensive advocacy and support tailored to your family's needs.

How We Help

Expert SEND Support Services

We offer a range of services designed to help families navigate the SEND system with confidence.

📝

EHCP Health Checks & Reviews

A thorough enforceability review of your child's EHC Plan. We check that needs are properly identified, provision is specific and quantified, and the plan would stand up to scrutiny — giving you annotated feedback, a needs-to-provision map, and a clear issues list you can act on immediately.

View pricing →
📋

EHC Needs Assessment Requests

A strong, evidence-based request for an EHC Needs Assessment — professionally drafted around the legal test and supported by a full evidence index. We make sure your request is as robust as possible before it reaches the local authority.

View pricing →
📅

Annual Review Support

Comprehensive preparation, attendance, and follow-up for your child's annual review. We help you set an agenda focused on real outcomes, provide speaking notes, attend the meeting alongside you (virtually or in person), and ensure agreed actions don't get lost.

View pricing →
⚖

Tribunal Preparation & Representation

From lodging your appeal to attending the hearing, we support families through every stage of the SEND Tribunal process. This includes drafting grounds of appeal, full case management, evidence scheduling, working documents, LA correspondence, and hearing attendance as your named representative.

View pricing →
🤝

Mediation & Meeting Attendance

An experienced advocate alongside you in meetings with schools, the local authority, or during mediation sessions. We attend virtually or in person, advocate on your behalf in real time, and provide a follow-up summary so nothing agreed gets overlooked.

View pricing →
🏠

EOTAS & Alternative Provision

Bespoke Education Otherwise Than At School packages for young people who cannot attend mainstream settings. We coordinate multi-disciplinary teams, design personalised provision, and build timetables and costings for learners with complex medical needs and neurodivergence.

View pricing →
💬

Specialist SEND Advice

Flexible, desk-based support for families who need guidance on a specific question or situation. This includes advice calls, document drafting, EHCP and report analysis, evidence organisation, and written guidance — all billed hourly with no long-term commitment.

View pricing →
⚠

Disability Discrimination & Complex Casework

Specialist support for disability discrimination claims brought through the SEND Tribunal, as well as complex cases involving cease-to-maintain decisions, extended appeal elements, and pre-action protocol preparation. We set clear scope boundaries and signpost to solicitors where appropriate.

View pricing →
