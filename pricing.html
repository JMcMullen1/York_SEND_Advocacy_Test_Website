Tribunal Preparation & Case Support

For families who need us beyond the Kickstart stage. Tribunal cases can range from 15 to over 100 hours — our capped pricing means you'll never pay more than the agreed maximum, and if your case settles early, billing stops.

Appeal Type Typical Hours Capped Fee What This Covers
Refusal to Assess (SEND35A) ~20 hours £2,950 Full case management from lodging to hearing. Usually paper-based with focused evidence.
Refusal to Issue an EHCP ~30 hours £4,250 Case management, evidence scheduling, working document, and LA correspondence to hearing.
Contents Appeal — B & F ~40 hours £5,950 Needs and provision disputes. Full directions compliance, working document, and submissions.
Contents & Placement — B, F & I ~40+ hours £5,950 As above, plus school suitability evidence and placement-specific submissions.
Placement Only — Section I Variable £4,250 Focused on school suitability. Risk of scope expansion where provision is also disputed.
Cease to Maintain ~50 hours £6,750 Evidence of ongoing need is central. Often involves transition planning disputes.
Disability Discrimination Claim ~50 hours £6,950 Must be brought within 6 months of the discrimination. Clear scope boundaries advised.
EOTAS Package Building (tribunal) Variable £6,950 Specialist rate applies. Includes timetable, costing, and package-building work.
Extended Appeal Element (health/social care) +5-10 hours +£650 Add-on to any appeal. Non-binding recommendations on health and social care provision.

All capped fees exclude hearing attendance, which is priced separately below. Caps cover case management, directions compliance, evidence scheduling, working document iterations, submissions, and all LA/tribunal correspondence. If your case settles before the cap is reached, billing stops.

How hourly billing works

Most work is billed in 15-minute increments with a minimum of 1 hour on new matters. Where preparation is required for meeting attendance (reviewing documents, drafting agendas, etc.), prep time is charged at the standard rate of £90/hour unless included within a fixed-fee package. Travel time and mileage are charged separately for locations outside the York local area.
