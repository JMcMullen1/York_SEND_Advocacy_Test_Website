About Us

Our Story

York SEND Advocacy was founded in 2020 by a group of parents, educators, and legal professionals who recognized the need for independent support for families navigating the SEND system in York. What began as a small volunteer effort has grown into a trusted organisation helping dozens of families each year. We are rooted in the local community and driven by a passion to ensure every child's needs are met and rights respected.

Meet the Team

Jane Smith – Founder & Lead Advocate

Jane is a former SEN teacher and legal advocate with 15 years of experience. She founded York SEND Advocacy to empower families after her own experiences navigating the system.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity: We welcome all backgrounds and use respectful, person-centered language so every family feels heard and respected.
  • Empowerment: We equip parents and young people with knowledge of their SEND rights, enabling them to make informed decisions and advocate confidently.
  • Compassion: We approach every case with empathy, understanding the challenges families face and providing steady moral support throughout the process.
  • Integrity: Our service is impartial and confidential – we always act in the child's best interest and give honest, transparent advice.
