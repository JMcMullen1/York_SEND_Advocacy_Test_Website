About Us

Meet the team behind York SEND Advocacy and discover what drives our mission.

Our Story

Independent SEND Support in York

York SEND Advocacy exists because navigating the SEND system shouldn't be something families have to do alone. We provide independent advice and hands-on support to help parents and carers secure the right provision for their children.

Our team brings together professional expertise in SEND law, education, and advocacy. We work directly with families across York and the surrounding areas, guiding them through EHCPs, tribunals, and everything in between.

We are completely independent — we don't work for any local authority or school. Our only focus is getting the best outcome for your child.

Our Core Values

The principles that inform everything we do for the families we serve.

♥

Inclusivity

We welcome all backgrounds and use respectful, person-centred language so every family feels heard and respected.

★

Empowerment

We equip parents and young people with knowledge of their SEND rights, enabling them to make informed decisions and advocate confidently.

♡

Compassion

We approach every case with empathy, understanding the challenges families face and providing steady moral support throughout the process.

⚙

Integrity

Our service is impartial and confidential — we always act in the child's best interest and give honest, transparent advice.
