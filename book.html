What to Expect

Your initial consultation is completely free and lasts approximately 30 minutes. We will discuss your situation, explain how we can help, and outline the next steps.

📞

Flexible Options

Appointments can be held in person at our York office, over the phone, or via video call — whichever suits you best.

🔒

Confidential & Safe

Everything you share with us is treated in the strictest confidence. We are here to support you in a safe, non-judgemental environment.
