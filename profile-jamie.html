SEND Advocate

Experienced. Independent. Family-Focused.

I have worked in SEND for over 10 years, and my route into it was a bit unconventional. I originally set up a boxing gym in Bradford, which grew into an award-winning Alternative Provision supporting young people who were struggling in mainstream education. From there, I moved into consultancy for Alternative Provisions and went on to lead multiple EOTAS-style provisions, alongside working as a SEND consultant and advocate supporting families through EHCPs, appeals, and wider disputes with Local Authorities.

That journey — from grassroots youth work to independent advocacy — gives me a thorough understanding of how the SEND system operates, and where it fails families. I have worked both within Local Authorities and as an independent consultant, and I have supported families through some of the most complex cases, including those involving medical needs, neurodivergence, school refusal and exploitation. My approach is grounded in SEND law, driven by empathy, and focused entirely on achieving the right outcomes for children and young people.

I am completely independent. I do not work for any Local Authority, school or health provider. My only interest is ensuring your child receives what the law says they should.

What I Do

SEND Advocacy Expertise

Core areas where I support families navigating the SEND system.

📝

EHCPs & Annual Reviews

Writing, reviewing and quality-assuring Education, Health and Care Plans to ensure they are legally robust, accurately reflect your child's needs, and specify the right provision. I have led annual review projects placed in the top quarter nationally.

⚖

Tribunals & Mediation

Representing and supporting families through SEND Tribunal appeals and mediation sessions. I have direct experience of Tribunal casework and resolving disputes with Local Authorities, with a focus on achieving lawful outcomes.

🏠

EOTAS & Alternative Provision

Leading bespoke Education Otherwise Than At School packages for young people who cannot attend mainstream settings. I coordinate multi-disciplinary teams and design personalised provision for learners with complex medical needs and neurodivergence.

👥

Family Advocacy & Empowerment

Helping families understand their legal rights under the Children and Families Act 2014 and the SEND Code of Practice. I equip parents with the knowledge and confidence to advocate effectively for their children.

💪

Bespoke 1:1 Interventions

Providing personalised therapeutic and educational interventions for EOTAS learners with complex needs, including trauma histories, school refusal and anxiety-based presentations.

✅

Safeguarding & Complex Casework

Designated Safeguarding Lead experience working with cases involving exploitation, domestic abuse and trauma. Experienced with CIN, CP processes, and the intersection of SEND and safeguarding.

My Approach

What I Bring to Your Family

I understand how overwhelming and isolating the SEND system can feel, particularly when you have been let down before. Many of the families I work with have experienced years of inadequate provision, broken promises, and a system that feels stacked against them.

I bring deep SEND law expertise, practical Tribunal and mediation experience, and a proven ability to hold Local Authorities to account. I combine this with genuine empathy and a commitment to person-centred, lawful practice.

Whether you need help understanding an EHCP, preparing for a Tribunal, or designing an EOTAS package that actually works for your child, I will work alongside you to make sure the system delivers what it should.
